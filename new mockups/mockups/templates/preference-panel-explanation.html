Accessible via our Mobile Service

        Mobile airport service will navigate you at your Airport, so you don’t get lost on your way to the gate and your interests are respected. By choosing one of the stated preferences you will receive a route to your points of interests with calculated time and a comparison with the time you need to get to your gate.





The first chosen airport will be a default airport in your booking process; you will be able to easily change it during the process. If we notice that you more often fly from another airport, we will change it for you, the same for destination airport.

Your travel companions information will be displayed in your booking process. This will easy your booking process, if you want to book more than one ticket.

Dream destinations are your wishes about your vacation time, we will take them into consideration and you will receive information about possibilities to make your dream come true via email and other touchpoints

Passengers who require special food for dietary, nutritional or religious reasons have a choice of 16 different meals on EUAir operated flights.

Special meals can be ordered up to 24 hours before departure, provided that there is a confirmed booking in the required class. You have the opportunity to order a special meal through your travel agent or online under My Bookings. There will be no additional costs for the passenger for this service.

Please note that special meals are usually offered in Economy Class on flights of more than 185 minutes duration and in Business Class on flights of more than 75 minutes duration.
